Fraudulent loans with forged documents
Modus Operandi
➢ Fraudsters use forged documents to avail services from financial institutions.
➢ Fraudsters commit identity thefts, steal personal information of customers such as
identity cards, bank account details etc., and use this information or credentials to avail
benefits from a financial institution.
➢ Fraudsters pose as NBFC employees and collect KYC related documents from
customers.
Precautions
➢ Exercise due care and vigilance while providing KYC and other personal documents, including
the National Automated Clearing House (NACH) form for loan sanction / availing of credit
facility from any entity, especially individuals posing to be representatives of these entities.
