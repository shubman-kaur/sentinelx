10. Impersonation on social media
Modus Operandi
➢ Fraudsters create fake accounts using details of the users of social media platforms
such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.
➢ Fraudsters then send a request to the users’ friends asking for money for urgent
medical purposes, payments, etc.
➢ Fraudsters, using fake details, also contact users and gain users’ trust over a period
of time.
