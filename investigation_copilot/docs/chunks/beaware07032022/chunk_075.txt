➢ EMI: It stands for Equated Monthly Instalment. This a fixed monthly payment (includes
principal and interest) to be made by a borrower to his lender/creditor (like bank/NBFC)
each month till the loan/credit, along with interest, taken from the lender/creditor is paid off
by the borrower in full.
➢ Encryption: The process of transforming processing information into an electronic code
to maintain its secrecy.
➢ Expiry date: The date on which the validity of a card, contract, agreement, document, etc.
expires.
