➢ Card number: The number assigned by a credit card association or card issuing bank to
a card. This information must be provided to a merchant by a customer in order to make
a credit card payment but should not be shared with anyone else. The string of digits is
printed on the card.
➢ Credit card: A card that allows paying for products or services by availing
unsecured/secured credit from a financial institution.
➢ Credit limit: The term refers to the maximum amount of credit a financial institution
extends to a customer. A lending institution extends a credit limit on a credit card based
on the analysis of the information given by the credit-seeking applicant.
