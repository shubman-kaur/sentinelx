How do you know whether an NBFC accepting deposit is genuine or not?
➢ Verify whether the name of NBFC appears in the list of deposit taking NBFCs entitled
to accept deposits, available at https://rbi.org.in and to ensure that it is not appearing
in the list of companies prohibited from accepting deposits.
➢ NBFCs must prominently display the Certificate of Registration (CoR) issued by the
Reserve Bank on its site / in its office. This certificate should also reflect that the NBFC
has been specifically authorised by RBI to accept deposits.
