1. Fake advertisements for extending loans by fraudsters
Modus Operandi
➢ Fraudsters issue fake advertisements offering personal loans at very attractive and low
rates of interest or easy repayment options or without any requirement of collateral/
security, etc.
➢ Fraudsters send emails with such offers and ask the borrowers to contact them.
