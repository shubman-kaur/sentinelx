3. OTP based Frauds
Modus Operandi
➢ Fraudsters impersonating as NBFCs, send SMS / messages offering loans or
enhancement of credit limit on NBFC/bank customers’ loan accounts, and ask
the customers to contact them on a mobile number.
➢ When the customers call such numbers, fraudsters ask them to fill forms to collect their
financial credentials.
