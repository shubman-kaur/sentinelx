Precautions
➢ Do not allow others to use your account to receive or transfer money for a fee / payment.
➢ Do not respond to emails asking for your bank account details.
➢ Do not get carried away by attractive offers / commissions and give consent to receive
unauthorised money and to transfer them to others or withdraw cash and give it out for a
handsome fee.
➢ If the source of funds is not genuine, or the rationale for underlying transaction is not proved
to authorities, the receiver of money is likely to land in serious trouble with police and other
law enforcement agencies.
