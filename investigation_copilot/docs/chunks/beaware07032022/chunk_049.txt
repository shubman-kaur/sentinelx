5. Money circulation / Ponzi / Multi-Level Marketing (MLM)
schemes fraud
Modus Operandi
➢ Fraudsters use MLM / Chain Marketing / Pyramid
Structure schemes to promise easy or quick
money upon enrolment / adding of members.
➢ The schemes not only assure high returns but also
pay the first few instalments (EMIs) to gain
confidence of gullible persons and attract more
investors through word of mouth publicity.
➢ The schemes encourage addition of more people
to the chain / group.
