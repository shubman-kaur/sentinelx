The Accusation
Targets are falsely accused of carrying out illegal activity, and
they are threatened using legal jargon and fake case numbers.
Scare Tactics and Urgency
Scammers pressure victims to act fast by threatening arrest or
fines. If questioned, they behave aggressive and make harsher
threats to scare the victim into complying.
