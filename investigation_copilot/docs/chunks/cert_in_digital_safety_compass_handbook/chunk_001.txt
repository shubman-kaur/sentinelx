PREFACE
The Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In)
is a Government Organization under the Ministry of
Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government
of India established with the objective of securing Indian
cyber space. CERT-In provides Incident Prevention and
Response services as well as Security Quality Management
Services.
CERT-In has been designated to serve as the national
agency for incident response under Section 70B of the
Information Technology Act, 2000 (Amendment 2008).
