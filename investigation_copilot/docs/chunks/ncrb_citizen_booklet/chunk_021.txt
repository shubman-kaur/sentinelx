Do not allow the sales person to take your card
away to swipe for the transaction.
• Look out for credit/debit card skimmers anywhere you swipe your card,
especially at petrol pumps, ATMs etc.
• If you notice a credit/debit card reader that protrudes outside the face of the rest
of the machine, it may be a skimmer.
• Never share your PIN with anybody, however close they might be.
