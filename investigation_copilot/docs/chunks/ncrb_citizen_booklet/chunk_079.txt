Let us look at some examples on how our personal computer can get affected by virus.
Virus Attack through external devices
A virus can enter the computer through external devices like pen drive or hard disk etc. This virus
can spread across all the computer files.
Virus Attack by downloading files from un-trusted websites
The virus can enter the computer by download of files from un-trusted websites. The virus can be
hidden in the form of music files, video files or any attractive advertisement.
