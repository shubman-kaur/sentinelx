As the services are shifting towards online platforms, cyber frauds related to banking are also
increasing. Just like we protect our locker full of jewelry with a lock and key, we must protect our
online bank accounts with strong passwords. If the key is stolen, then the jewelry will be stolen.
Similarly, if the password is stolen, then the money in the bank accounts will be stolen.
