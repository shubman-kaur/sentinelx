What is Identity Theft?
Identity theft is the act of wrongfully obtaining someone’s personal information (that defines one’s
identity) without their permission. The personal information may include their name, phone number,
address, bank account number, Aadhaar number or credit/debit card number etc.
Identity theft can have many adverse effects.
