Credit/Debit Card Skimming
Credit/Debit card skimming is done using a small device called skimmer. The magnetic stripe of the
card stores details such as name, credit/debit card number and expiration date. First, the credit/
debit card is swiped through a skimmer. Then, the skimmer captures all these details. Thieves use
this stolen data to make online transactions.
