Cyber Stalking
Cyber stalking is a crime in which the attacker harasses a victim using electronic communication,
such as e-mail, instant messaging (IM), messages posted on a website or a discussion group.
A cyber stalker relies upon the fact that his/her true identity is not known in the digital world.
A cyber stalker targets the victim with threatening/abusive messages and follows them/their
activities in the real world.
Cyber Bullying
Cyber bullying is bullying that takes place over digital devices.
