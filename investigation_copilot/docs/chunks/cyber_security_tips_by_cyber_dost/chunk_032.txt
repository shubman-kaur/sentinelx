5.3. Be careful while using public Wi-Fi at Airports, Railway Stations, Bus
Stops etc. Public Wi-Fi is an easy target for any hacker to steal your
information. Use secure VPN or proxy to avoid unauthorised access to
your personal information.
5.4. Consider using the Media Access Control, or "MAC," address filter in
your wireless router. Every electronic device that can connect to a Wi-
Fi network has a unique ID called the "physical address" or "MAC"
address. Wireless router can screen the MAC addresses of all devices
connected to it, and users can set their wireless network to accept
connections only from devices with MAC addresses recognized by
router.
