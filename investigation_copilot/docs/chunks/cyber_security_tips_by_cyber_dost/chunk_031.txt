5. Wireless / Bluetooth security awareness
5.1. Secure all the wireless access points (Wi-Fi, Routers) with a strong
password. Hackers usually scan for open access points, a method called
as war-driving to anonymize their identity.
5.2. Be cautious while browsing through a public Wi-Fi and avoid logging in
to personal & professional accounts like e-mail, banking transactions
on these networks.
