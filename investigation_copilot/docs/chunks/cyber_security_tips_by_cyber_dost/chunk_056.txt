9.32 Parents should guide their children on safe use of social networking
platforms, chat rooms, instant messaging, SMS & mobile phones. This
will help in protecting children against serious threats such as cyber-
bullying, identity theft, etc.
9.33 "Report & Block” any message that asks you to harm yourself.
Immediately inform your parents/ elders about such messages and
report the incident to police.
9.34 Parents should monitor their children’s usage of social media and
should be vigilant about any abrupt changes in their behaviour.
9.35 Do not add unknown contacts challenging, daring or scaring you
through messages.
