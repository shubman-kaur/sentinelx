4.2.4. Protecting your email & social media accounts is important as any
breach can result in loss of personal data. Always use two factor
authentication features (password & verification code on your
registered mobile) to secure your email/social media accounts.
4.2.5. When accessing online accounts via public computers ensure that
remember me / save password is not selected when prompted.
4.2.6. In the password recovery settings do not set the questions which
can be easily answered or identified from your social media accounts
like date of birth, first school name etc.
